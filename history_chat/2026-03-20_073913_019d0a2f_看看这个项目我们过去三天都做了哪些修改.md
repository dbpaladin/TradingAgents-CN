这是为什么后面一直报 `NO_PUBKEY 41DE058A4E7DCA05`。

```bash

  --keyring /usr/share/keyrings/mongodb-server-8.0.gpg \
  --keyserver hkps://keyserver.ubuntu.com \
  --recv-keys 41DE058A4E7DCA05

ls -l /usr/share/keyrings/mongodb-server-8.0.gpg

sudo apt-get update
apt-cache policy mongodb-org redis-server
```
